#StepWithRefugees

Responding to needs of Cameroonian Refugees as well as IDPs due to the on-going ‘Armed Conflict’ in the Anglophone regions of the country!

Humanitarian

Humanitarian response to both IDPs and refugees is at an alerting stages as there is great need for food, shelter, clothing and relief materials.

Livelihood

To support livelihood activities of host communities and refugees to increase their incomes and expand their economic opportunities.

Digital

By 'digital response' to the needs of refugees, the concept is base on ICT4Refugees in providing low access ICT technologies to better lifes of refugees.
